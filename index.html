Knowing the number of people that access a given building, is very essential in planning for the masses that access the building. In cases of emergencies like fire outbreaks or terrorist attacks, it is essential to make sure that every individual who was in the building when a given unfortunate event ocurred is accounted for.

The bi-directional counter at the CIT block B building at Makerere university will help generate a daily record of the number of people that access the building.
